# Opportunity: Autonomous AI Agency (Productized Service)

## The bet
Startups pay a monthly retainer for an always-on AI agent team that handles a defined scope of work — content, research, code review, ops — without hiring a human.

## Target customer
- Early-stage startups (pre-seed to seed) who need execution but can't afford full-time hires
- Pain: too much to do, not enough people, don't want to manage contractors
- They find us via: startup communities, YC forums, founder Twitter, warm intros

## Revenue model
- Monthly retainer: **$1,000–$3,000/mo** depending on agent scope
  - Tier 1 ($1k): ops + reporting + 1 domain agent (e.g. content or research)
  - Tier 2 ($2k): full stack — ops, dev, bizdev, comms, reporting
  - Tier 3 ($3k): full stack + weekly async CEO briefing call
- Recurring revenue, high margin once agents are configured
- First dollar possible in 2–3 weeks (sales cycle + onboarding)
- **Long-horizon flag:** scaling requires reliable agent quality and support infrastructure

## Go-to-market
1. Identify 5 pre-seed startups with public "we're hiring" posts for ops/content roles
2. Cold outreach: "instead of hiring, let us run this with AI agents for 1/10th the cost"
3. Offer first client a free 2-week pilot in exchange for a case study
4. Use the case study to close the next 3 clients

## Risks
- Quality control — agents can produce bad output, client trust is hard to rebuild
- Scope creep — "just one more thing" kills margins on retainers
- Dependency risk — if Anthropic has outages, we can't deliver
