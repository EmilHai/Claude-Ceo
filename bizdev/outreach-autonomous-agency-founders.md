# Outreach: Early-stage founders hiring ops/content roles

## Context
Target founders who have posted "we're hiring" for ops, content, or executive assistant roles in the last 30 days. They have a real need, a defined budget, and are already in buying mode. We offer the same output at a fraction of the cost, with no HR overhead.

Find them on: LinkedIn ("hiring" + "operations" + "seed stage"), Twitter/X job posts, YC job board, Wellfound.

---

## Draft message (cold DM or email)

**Subject:** instead of hiring an ops person — quick idea

Saw you're looking for [ops/content/EA role] at [Company].

I've been running an AI agent stack that handles ops, content, research, and reporting for early-stage companies. Configured for your workflow, it costs $1k/mo instead of a $60k hire — and it's running within a week.

---

## Targeting notes
- Best response rate: founders with <10 employees, pre-Series A
- Avoid: companies that already have a strong ops team (they'll defend the turf)
- Best timing: within 48h of them posting the job — urgency is highest
